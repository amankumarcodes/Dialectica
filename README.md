# Dialectica: AI Debate Partner

Dialectica is a high-performance rhetorical training platform designed to sharpen critical thinking and public speaking skills through artificial intelligence. Built on the Google Gemini API, it offers a multi-modal environment for rigorous intellectual discourse.

### 1. The Arena (Text & Voice)
*   **Structured Discourse**: Engage in turn-based debates on a variety of pre-set or custom topics.
*   **Logical Fallacy Detection**: Real-time identification of common rhetorical errors (Ad Hominem, Strawman, etc.).
*   **Argument Scoring**: Instant feedback on the strength and clarity of your logic.
*   **Voice Dictation**: High-accuracy speech-to-text powered by Gemini for hands-free argument drafting.

### 2. Live Vocal Rhetoric
*   **Gemini Live Integration**: Natural, low-latency voice conversations with a professional AI debater.
*   **Dynamic Interruptions**: A fluid experience where the model can be interrupted, just like a real debate.
*   **Live Transcription**: Visual feedback of the conversation as it happens.

### 3. AI-Powered Lobby
*   **Magic Refine**: Enter any raw topic idea, and Dialectica will transform it into a professional debate prompt with context and categorization.
*   **Side Selection**: Choose to defend (PRO) or challenge (CON) any position.

### 4. Analysis Dashboard
*   **Rhetorical Profile**: A radar chart visualizing your logic, evidence, clarity, rhetoric, and courtesy.
*   **Performance Trends**: Track your argument strength over multiple rounds.
*   **Vulnerability Tracking**: See which logical fallacies you rely on most often.
